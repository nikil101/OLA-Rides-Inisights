# 🚖 OLA Ride Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline

**OLA Ride Analytics Dashboard**

A dynamic and interactive analytics dashboard built using SQL, Python, Power BI, and Streamlit to analyze ride booking trends, revenue performance, customer behavior, cancellation patterns, and operational efficiency across OLA ride services.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The OLA Ride Analytics Dashboard is a comprehensive business intelligence solution designed to help stakeholders understand ride demand patterns, revenue generation, booking success rates, customer satisfaction, and cancellation behavior. The dashboard transforms raw ride-booking data into actionable insights that support operational and business decision-making.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The project was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for dashboard development.
* 🗄️ **MySQL** – Data storage, querying, relationship management, and business analysis.
* 🐍 **Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)** – Data cleaning, preprocessing, and exploratory data analysis.
* 🚀 **Streamlit** – Interactive web application for exploring ride analytics.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for KPI calculations and dynamic reporting.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Relationships established between ride-related datasets to support analysis.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Data transformation and preparation layer.
* 📁 **CSV Datasets** – Source data for ride transactions and operational analysis.

---

## 4. Data Source

The dashboard utilizes an OLA ride-booking dataset containing more than 100,000 ride records with information such as:

* Booking Status
* Vehicle Type
* Ride Distance
* Customer Ratings
* Driver Ratings
* Payment Method
* Revenue Generated
* Cancellation Reasons
* Ride Date
* Ride Value

The dataset was cleaned, transformed, and modeled to support business-focused analysis and reporting.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Ride-hailing platforms generate massive amounts of operational data every day. However, understanding ride demand, revenue trends, customer experience, and cancellation behavior becomes difficult without a centralized analytics solution.

Key business questions include:

* Which vehicle categories generate the highest demand?
* What are the major causes of ride cancellations?
* Which payment methods contribute most to revenue?
* How successful are ride bookings?
* How satisfied are customers across vehicle categories?
* What trends exist in ride demand over time?

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard

To deliver an interactive analytics solution that:

* Enables monitoring of ride demand and booking performance.
* Tracks revenue generation and business growth.
* Identifies customer and driver cancellation patterns.
* Evaluates customer satisfaction across vehicle categories.
* Supports operational improvements through data-driven insights.
* Helps stakeholders make informed business decisions.

### • Business Impact & Insights

#### Prime Vehicle Categories Drive Demand

Prime Sedan and Prime SUV emerged as some of the most utilized vehicle categories, indicating strong customer preference for premium ride options.

#### Digital Payments Dominate Revenue

UPI and Cash contributed the highest share of total revenue, highlighting customer payment behavior and transaction trends.

#### Driver Cancellations Impact Service Performance

Driver-related cancellations represented a significant portion of unsuccessful bookings, indicating opportunities for operational improvements.

#### Customer Ratings Remain Consistent

Average customer ratings remained close to 4.0 across vehicle categories, indicating a generally positive customer experience.

#### Ride Demand Shows Daily Variations

Ride volume fluctuated throughout the month, helping identify peak demand periods and potential resource allocation opportunities.

#### Cancellation Analysis Reveals Operational Challenges

Both customer and driver cancellation patterns highlighted areas where service quality and ride allocation processes could be optimized.

---

## 6. Key Takeaways

* Prime Sedan and Prime SUV were among the most preferred vehicle categories.
* UPI and Cash generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Customer ratings remained consistently positive across vehicle types.
* Driver-related cancellations represented a key operational challenge.
* Ride demand fluctuated throughout the month, indicating changing customer requirements.
* Data-driven decision-making can significantly improve ride allocation and service efficiency.

---

## 7. Conclusion

The OLA Ride Analytics Dashboard transforms raw ride-booking data into meaningful business intelligence. By analyzing ride demand, revenue generation, customer satisfaction, vehicle performance, and cancellation behavior, the dashboard enables stakeholders to identify operational improvements, optimize resources, and enhance customer experience through data-driven decision-making.
